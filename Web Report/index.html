Introduction

Our project is about finding the best shape for roofs based on coverage area. We embarked on this project after discovering that, depite the sheer imporatance of roofs, few sources help engineers decide what roof shape to use that best shelters us.

Our aim is to use mathematical algorithms to find the best shape for water runoff such that the water lands at the furthest distance from the edge of the roof. We will be using a few common geometric shapes such as triangles of different angles and the dome.

Limitations

Due to the complexity of our project, we have a number of limitations.

The raindrop:
-Has a diameter of 6mm
-Is perfectly spherical
-Has an initial velocity of 0
-Has an initial position at the top of the roof
-Has a terminal velocity of 10m/s
-Has no friction against the air of roof
